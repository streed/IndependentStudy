About:

What is this?

RoaRide is an advanced ride board that allows for riders to select from a pool of available drivers based on time, start address, and end address. As well as this ease of selecting and finding a ride the system brings both parties together with very little effort from both ends.

Why?

It is always frustrating to find rides and even more frustrating when you know someone was just where you wanted to go, but you didn't know they could give you a ride. So, making a system that alleviates this solves a very frustrating problem that typical ride sharing boards do not effectively solve.
